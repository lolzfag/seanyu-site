I've been meaning to start writing for a while. The reason is simple: **content compounds**. Every founder, investor, and operator I respect writes in public — and the ones who don't are leaving authority on the table.

## What I'll write about

A few things I think about most days:

- Building **Gingercontrol** (AI-native trade compliance) and **Peony** (modern data rooms)
- SaaS go-to-market — PLG, content, cold outreach, pricing
- Venture capital — from the inside, having spent time at Backed VC and Target Global
- SEO and GEO — how to build organic discovery that works in 2026 and beyond

I'll keep the tone direct and the posts short-ish. No listicles, no LinkedIn-isms.

## Why MDX

This blog is written in [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/) — markdown that lets me drop React components inline. Practical example: if I want to embed a chart, a live demo, or a custom callout, I just write it like an HTML element. No SEO penalty — the final HTML is the same as any other page. The advantage is purely authoring ergonomics.
